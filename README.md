## 🖥️ How to Use testing-business-ideas-with-claude

The app takes you through the business testing process in three parts:

### 1. Extract  
Type or paste your business idea into the app. The app will help you identify key parts, such as the customer group, value proposition, and market problem.

### 2. Map  
The tool creates a visual map of your business idea’s main components. This map shows how different parts link together. You can edit or add details to make the idea clearer.

### 3. Test  
Use this part to create simple tests for your idea. The app suggests questions to ask potential customers or ways to validate the value your idea provides. You can save your test plans inside the app.

Each step is designed to be easy and clear, even if you have no prior experience with business planning or software tools.
